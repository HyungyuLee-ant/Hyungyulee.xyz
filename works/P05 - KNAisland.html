Description

KNAIsland is a game-type education metaverse on Roblox platform where Generation Alpha can learn the basic principles of blockchain through experience. Players can easily learn each part and element of block chain system - mining, chaining, community activities, previous mining records, etc. These elements are expressed metaphorically as a fun KNA economy quest, thus facilitating users with the concept of blockchain through ‘learning by doing.’

KNA Island was invited for showcase at the 11th Art & Technology Conference.

Our group, Team Knagom is made of three artists who are studying Art & Technology and Computer Science. Knagom is making its own world, ‘KNA Island’, where no one is suffering from chronic back and neck pain, on Roblox platform.

As a team leader of Team Knagom, I was in charge of documentation and admistrative works regarding the projects and fundings from Sogang University and University Innovation Program. I was also in charge of design and development of several key features of the project.

Jina Kim was in Charge of Planning of our economic model and blockchain.
