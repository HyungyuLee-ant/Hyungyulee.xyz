Find Your B612

Fall 2019

Sentimental Interactive Art using Mouse

Intro to Creative Computing Weekly Project

P5.js

Inspiration

B612 is a special star, from which the little prine of Le Petit Prince by *Antoine de Saint-Exupéry* is from. Since Le Petit Prince is a storybook that is familiar to eveyone, I wanted to make user such sentimental expereince of finding their own B612 in the vast universe. With thousands of dots, which resembles the number of stars at the universe, the user can use arrow key to move the stars faster/slower, and download the canvas image.

If the user clicks on the canvas, stars near mouseX will disappear, and the user’s own B612 will be shown.

Try it out and find your own B612!
